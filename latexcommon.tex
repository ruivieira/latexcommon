\subsection{Distributions}

\subsubsection{\texttt{\textbackslash N}, Normal}

Generates a normal distribution with two parameters.
As an example,

\begin{lstlisting}[language=TeX]
\N{0, \sigma^2}
\end{lstlisting}

Will generate

\[ \N{0}{\sigma^2} \]
